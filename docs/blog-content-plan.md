# QuillCV Blog Content Plan

**Cadence**: 2 posts per month
- **Post A** — General: ATS education, region guides, industry tips, thought leadership
- **Post B** — DIY / Market: Free how-to guides or job market reality pieces

**Brand philosophy**: Give away real value. Readers who follow free advice and succeed become advocates. Readers who struggle become customers. Both outcomes win.

---

## Content Pillars

| Pillar | Purpose | Examples |
|--------|---------|---------|
| **ATS** | Demystify how ATS works | Keywords, formatting, scoring |
| **DIY Craft** | Teach people to build a strong CV manually | Step-by-step guides, checklists, before/after |
| **Region** | Country-specific CV conventions | AU vs US, photo rules, personal details |
| **Industry** | Sector and role-specific tips | Engineers, career changers, first jobs |
| **Market Reality** | Honest takes on the job search landscape | Networking, ghosting, certification traps |
