# QuillCV — Business Overview

## The Problem

Job seekers submit generic CVs that get filtered out by Applicant Tracking Systems before a human ever reads them. Tailoring a CV for each application is tedious, and most people don't know what ATS software looks for.

## What QuillCV Does

1. Takes the user's existing CV and a target job description
2. Extracts keywords, requirements, and skills the ATS will scan for
3. Finds similar-profile CVs online for structure and content inspiration
4. Generates a tailored, ATS-optimized CV that scores well with automated screening
5. Provides an ATS score, keyword gap analysis, and actionable recommendations

## Differentiators

- **Region-aware** — supports 12 country formats (AU, US, UK, CA, NZ, DE, FR, NL, IN, BR, AE, JP) with local conventions for photos, personal details, references, date formats, and page length
- **46 templates** — general-purpose (classic, modern, minimal, executive, tech, compact) and industry-specific (federal, healthcare, consulting, academic, legal, creative, engineering, and region-specific like europass, lebenslauf, rirekisho, curriculo)
- **Credit-based pricing** — no subscriptions, pay for what you use, credits never expire
- **Transparency** — shows exactly which keywords are missing and how to improve the ATS score

## Business Model

All prices in AUD. Payments via Stripe one-time purchases.
