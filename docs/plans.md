### QuillCV Differentiators

1. **Credit-based pay-per-use** — most competitors force subscriptions. Only Kickresume uses credits, but within a subscription. Our model is more flexible for occasional users.
2. **12 region-specific formats** — nobody else handles country-specific CV conventions (photo rules, date formats, section norms for AU, US, UK, DE, JP, etc.)
3. **Similar CV search** — scraping real CVs for structure and content inspiration is unique in this space.
4. **Apply suggestions workflow** — the "advanced" tier that applies fixes directly is a differentiator vs competitors that only show recommendations.

### Pricing Positioning

| | Cheapest Competitor | QuillCV Alpha | QuillCV Post-Alpha | Most Expensive Competitor |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Per use | — | $0.60 | $1.00–2.00 | — |
| Monthly equivalent | Kickresume $8/mo | ~$0.60 × usage | ~$15–30 for 10–20 CVs | Teal $29/mo, Rezi $29/mo |

Our credit-based model undercuts subscriptions for light users (1–5 CVs/month) while remaining competitive for heavier users at bulk rates.
