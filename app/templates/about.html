About QuillCV

QuillCV is built for people who are serious about landing the right job. We use AI to tailor your CV to each job description — matching keywords, formatting for your target country, and structuring content the way Applicant Tracking Systems expect to see it.

Why we built this

Most CV builders give you a template and leave you to fill it in. That's fine for design, but it doesn't help you pass ATS filters. We saw too many qualified people getting rejected by automated systems because their CV didn't match the job description's keywords and structure.

QuillCV solves this by analyzing the job posting, extracting what the ATS is scanning for, and rewriting your CV to match — while keeping your real experience and achievements front and center.

What makes us different

  • Job-specific tailoring — not one generic CV, but a version optimized for each application
  • 12 country formats — Australia, US, UK, Canada, New Zealand, Germany, France, Netherlands, India, Brazil, UAE, and Japan each have different conventions
  • ATS keyword matching — we extract and score keywords from the job description automatically
  • Quality review — AI flags weak sections, irrelevant skills, and poor phrasing before you submit

Who's behind this

QuillCV is an independent product built in Australia. We're a small team focused on doing one thing well: helping you get past the ATS and into the interview.
