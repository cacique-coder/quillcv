Common questions

Do QuillCV credits expire?

No. Alpha credits never expire. Use them whenever you need, no pressure.

What counts as one CV generation?

One credit = one tailored CV generation. Refinements (tweaking an existing generated CV) will cost half a credit in a future update — for now, they're free.

What is a refinement?

After generating a CV, you can refine specific sections — adjusting tone, adding detail, or restructuring. During alpha, refinements are free. Post-alpha, they'll cost half a credit.

Can I buy more QuillCV credits later?

Yes! Top-up packs will be available after alpha. Credits never expire.

Are QuillCV templates free?

Yes — all templates are completely free. Sign up, pick one, and fill it in yourself at no cost. Credits are only used when AI generates a tailored CV for you.

Can I edit my CV manually after generation?

Absolutely, and it's free. Once your CV is generated you can edit the content directly — no credits used. Credits only cover the initial AI generation.
