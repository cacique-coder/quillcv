Common questions

Do QuillCV credits expire?

No. Alpha credits never expire. Use them whenever you need, no pressure.

What counts as one CV generation?

One generation = one tailored CV output. You can review, apply quality fixes, and download the PDF — all for one credit.

Can I buy more QuillCV credits later?

Yes! Top-up packs are available right now — 10, 25, or 50 credits. Credits never expire.

Are QuillCV templates free?

No — all templates are completely free. Sign up, pick one, and fill it in yourself at no cost. Credits are only used when AI generates a tailored CV for you.

Can I edit my CV manually after generation?

Absolutely, and it's free. Once your CV is generated you can edit the content directly — no credits used. Credits only cover the initial AI generation.
