{# ── FAQ ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────── #}

Honest questions, honest answers

because pricing pages should explain themselves
Why one-time and not a subscription?

Because CVs are punctuated work. You need them hard for a month, then maybe not again for a year. Charging monthly punishes the moments you're not job-hunting — which is also, probably, when your employer treats you well. We'd rather you pay once and come back when you need us.

What counts as a "credit"?

One AI generation — either a tailored CV or a cover letter. Editing, re-formatting, switching templates, and manual edits cost nothing. Duplicating an existing CV and tweaking it by hand costs nothing. Only fresh AI generations spend credits.

What's in the "alpha" tag?

We're in active build. Things change weekly. You'll see the occasional rough edge and get the occasional "we just shipped this, try it" email. In return, you get 72% off, a direct line to the team, and the permanent 30% discount when we launch formal plans.

Can I get a refund?

Yes. If you haven't spent more than 3 credits, email us within 30 days and we'll refund the lot, no questions. If you're over 3 credits we'll pro-rate based on usage — because at that point, something worked enough for you to keep using it.

Will my credits expire?

Never. Alpha and top-up credits sit in your account until you use them. The only reason they'd disappear is if you close your account.

Is my data used to train AI models?

No. Your vault is encrypted client-side with a key derived from your password — we couldn't read it even if we wanted to. Job descriptions and CVs sent to the AI for a single generation are not retained or used for training, per our contract with the model providers. Full privacy policy →
