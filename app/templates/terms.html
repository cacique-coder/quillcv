8. Data protection commitment

We take the security and privacy of your CV content seriously. Our data protection commitments include:

  • Two-layer encryption — Your identity fields (name, address, contact details) are encrypted with a key derived from your password, giving you zero-knowledge protection for the most sensitive parts of your profile. Uploaded files are encrypted at rest using server-managed keys.

  • Temporary file cleanup — Files created during a CV generation session (uploaded source CVs, intermediate outputs) are automatically deleted after 7 days. You can also delete individual CVs and saved profiles at any time from your account.
