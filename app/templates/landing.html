{# ────────── FAQ ────────── #}

Questions worth asking.

The things people email us about before they sign up.

Will my CV still sound like me?

Yes. QuillCV matches your existing voice and only adds keywords when they're truthful to your experience. You can always edit, reject, or regenerate any part of the output.

Is my data actually private?

Yes. Your personal details are encrypted with your password — we physically can't read them. Delete your account and everything is gone, no 30-day grace period, no backups kept.

How is this different from ChatGPT or Claude?

ChatGPT doesn't know what an ATS scans for, doesn't handle 12 country conventions, and can't score your CV. QuillCV is purpose-built for the job application pipeline: ATS optimization, keyword extraction, country-correct formatting, and quality review — all in one place.

What counts as one "generation"?

One generation = tailoring your CV to one specific job description. If the first attempt needs tweaking, you can regenerate for free up to 3 times. Downloads are always unlimited.

Do you support my country?

Right now: Australia, US, UK, Canada, New Zealand, Germany, France, Netherlands, India, Brazil, UAE, and Japan. More are coming — tell us which one you need when you sign up.
